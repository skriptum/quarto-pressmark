***`P`ressmark*** - an elegant, typographic theme for Quarto websites. Inspired by newspapers like [The Atlantic](https://www.theatlantic.com/) and the [Financial Times](https://www.ft.com/) as well as [Edward Tufte’s design principles](https://edwardtufte.github.io/tufte-css/). Featuring good defaults, beatiful fonts, and a lot of customization. Made for sites centered around text, that do not want to compromise good design.
