



About the theme

- Fonts
  - [Playfair Display](https://github.com/clauseggers/Playfair)
  - [Josefin Sans](https://github.com/googlefonts/josefinsans)
- Color
  - slightly off white background
  - Adjustable, good defaults in light and dark mode
- Typographic elements 
  - Drop Caps, small caps, printers ornaments, etc.



Inspiration

- elegant news sites like [The Gothamist](https://gothamist.com/) and [The Financial Times](https://www.ft.com/)
- as well as classics like [Edward Tufte's design principles](https://edwardtufte.github.io/tufte-css/)





Features

- Markdown Kitchen Sink
- TOC and Banner
- Changing Colors (a.k.a a Dark Theme)
- Small Caps and Drop Caps
- Listings
- Citations
- Analytics
